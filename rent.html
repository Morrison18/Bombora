Rent Bombora

Equipment hire

If you have had a lesson before, come and put what you have learned to the test..!!

We rent Surfboards, Wetsuits, Boots and Boadyboards. All surfboards are foam slick bottom boards, we have 6ft, 7ft, 8ft and 9ft boards. We use C-Skins winter wetsuits (5/3), winter boots (5mm). When it gets colder we use hoods and gloves.

Our surf shop and surf school is located beside the beach in enniscrone. We're the massive Blue van directly beside the LifeGaurd tower.

WHAT KIND OF EQUIPMENT DO YOU RENT?

We rent everything from learner foam boards, beginner hard boards, epoxy beginner boards, epoxy shortboards & fibreglass shortboards. Our wetsuits are 5/3mm Billabong and C-Skins.

DO YOU RENT EVERYDAY?

We rent on most days unless the conditions are unsafe.

WHAT IS MEANT BY 1 DAY RENTAL?

Our prices are for a whole day's use so whether you're surfing for 1 hour or 8 the price is the same.

WHAT TIME CAN I DROP INTO THE SHOP TO RENT OR PICKUP MY RESERVED EQUIPMENT?

Our rentals department opens at 9am every morning. Best to get in early so that you have the entire day to use the equipment.

WHAT TIME DO I HAVE TO RETURN THE EQUIPMENT?

Equipment must be returned by 5.30pm on drop off date. Late return of rental equipment will incur a minimum penalty fee of one extra day’s rental. Damage to equipment will also incur an additional fee to cover repair or replacement, so please make sure you look after the equipment and return in the same state that you hired it in.
