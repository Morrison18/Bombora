About Us

We are based on The Surf Coast, right in the heart of Connaught's surfing capital - Enniscrone, Co. Sligo! We also have a rental service at Easkey peir, one of the best locations in Europe

Enniscrone is one of Ireland's premier sea side town. With the broad Atlantic to the west, the majestic Sligo-Leitrim mountains, Yeats country is ideally located for a countless number of attractions and entertainment.

Our surf shop and surf school is located beside the beach in enniscrone. We're the massive Blue van directly beside the LifeGaurd tower.
